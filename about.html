About this Tool

WC Vital is a tool that helps locate inclusive spaces such as publicly available gender-neutral bathrooms and free menstrual products in the local Chapel Hill-Carrboro area. This tool allows a multitude of different populations to gain access to a community that will foster charity and inclusivity.

Menstrual products are taxed as luxury goods when they should be treated as daily necessities so many populations lack proper access to products. Uses

The users of the website can find locations of places where they can find gender-neutral bathrooms and free menstrual products. The locations of the menstrual products would go both ways as people can donate and take products based on their needs. This would provide a solid spot where people can get products and leave them for others. And as for the bathrooms, this tool would pinpoint the exact locations of gender-neutral bathrooms in the area which is a convenient way to find these spaces before-hand.

Like Pearl Hacks, WC Vital strives to provide a supportive environment where people from different socio-economic backgrounds can come together and make a change for the larger population.
